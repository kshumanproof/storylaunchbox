FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions from storytellers.

Everything you need to know before submitting your screenplay to Story LaunchBox.

Who can enter?

Writers from any country and background are welcome to submit.

What stories fit?

We’re looking for original short scripts that make us feel something. Big swings. Weird ideas. Wild adventures. Intimate moments.

Stories that surprise us, wreck us, make us laugh, make us think, or remind us why we love storytelling in the first place.

How are submissions judged?

Scripts receive multiple professional readings before top projects are evaluated by experienced film and animation creatives connected to major studios and productions.

What happens to winners?

The winning screenplay moves into animated development, and finalist projects may also be considered for future opportunities.

Can others be selected?

Yes. Finalists may be considered for anthology inclusion, development conversations, or future production opportunities.

Can I submit more than one script?

Yes. Multiple submissions are permitted.

Do I keep the rights to my script?

Yes. Writers retain ownership of their work unless separate written agreements are executed for development or production.

What format should my script be in?

Scripts should follow standard screenplay formatting and be submitted as PDF files.

Can I update my script after submitting?

No. All submissions are final. Revised drafts must be submitted separately.

Is this a competition?

Story LaunchBox is a curated cinematic development initiative focused on discovering and producing emotionally powerful stories.

What is the page limit?

Screenplays should be between 5 and 25 pages.
