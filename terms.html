TERMS & CONDITIONS

Before your story enters the world.

Story LaunchBox was built to discover cinematic stories worth bringing to life. Before submitting, please review the participation terms, rights, and submission policies below.

Submission Guidelines
  • Original short scripts only
  • Maximum 25 pages in length
  • Properly formatted screenplays in PDF format (Final Draft is not required)
  • Writers must hold full rights to their submitted work
Important Dates
  • Submissions Open: June 1, 2026
  • Early Deadline: June 21, 2026
  • Regular Deadline: July 12, 2026
  • Final Deadline: August 2, 2026
  • Finalists Announced: August 23, 2026
  • Winner Announced: September 4, 2026
  • Development Phase Begins: End of 2026 / Beginning of 2027
Eligibility
  • Open internationally to writers 18 years or older
  • Multiple submissions are permitted
  • All submitted work must be wholly original
  • Adaptations require proof of underlying rights ownership
  • Scripts currently under option, acquisition, or active production elsewhere are not eligible
  • AI-generated scripts without meaningful human authorship are not eligible
Judging Criteria
  • Originality and emotional impact
  • Cinematic storytelling and visual imagination
  • Character depth and thematic resonance
  • Suitability for animated adaptation
  • Professional presentation and screenplay structure

Story LaunchBox reserves the right to determine finalist and winner selections at its sole discretion.

Rules & Terms
  • Entry fees are non-refundable
  • Story LaunchBox is not responsible for lost, corrupted, or incomplete submissions
  • Submission does not guarantee finalist selection, development, or production
  • Entrants may not submit defamatory, unlawful, or infringing material
  • Writers retain ownership unless separate written agreements are executed
  • Selected projects may require additional agreements for development and production
Rights & Agreements

Writers retain ownership of their submitted material.

If selected for development, Story LaunchBox may negotiate additional agreements related to:

  • Animated adaptation rights
  • Production participation
  • Festival distribution
  • Promotional usage
  • Sequels, expansions, or derivative works

No production relationship, partnership, or employment relationship is created through submission alone.
