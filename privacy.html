PRIVACY POLICY

Your stories stay yours.

Story LaunchBox respects your privacy, your creative ownership, and the trust involved in sharing your work.

Information We Collect

Story LaunchBox may collect personal information including names, email addresses, submission materials, and related communication data.

How We Use Information

Submitted information is used solely for screenplay evaluation, communication, development consideration, and operational administration.

Ownership & Confidentiality

Writers retain ownership of submitted work. Story LaunchBox does not claim ownership of submitted screenplays unless separate written agreements are executed.

Data Protection

Reasonable measures are taken to protect submitted information and materials, though no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

Submission platforms, payment processors, analytics providers, and external tools may operate under their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact Story LaunchBox through the official communication channels listed on the site.
